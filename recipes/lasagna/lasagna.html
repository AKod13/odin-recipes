How to Make Lasagna

  • Meat
  • Tomatoes
  • Bell peppers
  • Onions
  • Tomato juice
  • Cheese
  • Noodles

For this recipe, we are essentially making a thick, meaty tomato sauce and layering that with noodles and cheese into a casserole. Here's the run-down:

  1. Start by making the sauce with ground beef, bell peppers, onions, and a combo of tomato sauce, tomato paste, and crushed tomatoes
  2. Let this simmer while you boil the noodles and get the cheeses ready. We're using ricotta, shredded mozzarella, and parmesan.
  3. Assembly: A cup of meat sauce, a layer of noodles, more sauce, followed by a layer of cheese.
  4. Repeat assembly until you have three layers and have used up all the ingredients.
  5. Bake until bubbly and you're ready to eat!
